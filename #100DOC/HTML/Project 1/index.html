Are you trying to decide if you need more practice?

Why Should You Practice?

This is why. There are so many reasons why. There are thirteen reasons why. This is my thirteenth reason. If you ever want to amount to anything, you have to practice. If you do not practice, you do not fail. If you do not fail, you do not learn. Our greatest fear is not that we are inadequate, it is that we are powerful beyond measure. *mic drop*

Who Should Practice?

This is who:

  • Everyone
  • Anyone
  • Todos
Just sit down and practice something you're passionate about, dammit. Or something that can make you money. Put down the weed, turn off the tv, eliminate the distractions and just practice. Find your flow-state.

How Long Should You Practice?

This is how long. As long as it takes. As long as you can stand. As long as it's still sinking in. The science is still out on how long the average human can concentrate. Or maybe they're not. Eiher way, I'm sure they're skewing the results to align with the best interests of capitalism. Basically, just practice. Thirty minutes of practice everyday is better than three hours of practice once a week.

Tips For A Good Practice Sesh:

  • find some good study music--this will make/break your focus
  • make a comfortable study environment
  • bring your fully charged laptop & your chargers for worry-free study time

Snacks For A Good Practice Sesh:

  • coffee--caffeine is key
  • water--hydration nation over here, baby
  • brownies--or whatever brain food makes you smile
