Why Should You Practice?

This is why. There are so many reasons why. There are thirteen reasons why. This is my thirteenth reason. If you ever want to amount to anything, you have to practice. If you do not practice, you do not fail. If you do not fail, you do not learn. Our greatest fear is not that we are inadequate, it is that we are powerful beyond measure. *mic drop*

Who Should Practice?

This is who. Every one. Anyone? Todos. Just sit down and practice something you're passionate about, dammit. Or something that can make you money. Put down the weed, turn off the tv, eliminate the distractions and just practice. Find your flow-state.
